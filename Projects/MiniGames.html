Me and my father founded an FTC team who I built my first competition robot with.

The robot was co-designed with my team, and I did all of the cad.

The robot had an arm that moved up and down and extended, controlled by a spool, attached to it was a roller intake system for the pixels.

in total the robot took 3 months of dedicated work to complete; and work continued into competition day.
